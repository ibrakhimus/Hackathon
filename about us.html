About Us

Code Forward is a non-profit organization that teaches underprivileged youth how to code. We are a group of high school students who are passionate about teaching and coding. We are currently based in the Bay Area, but we hope to expand to other areas in the future. We are currently looking for volunteers to help us teach and mentor our students.
